Tennis Elbow (Lateral Epicondylitis)

Definition: Tennis elbow is a condition characterized by pain and inflammation on the outer part of the elbow, typically caused by overuse or repetitive strain.

    Physiotherapy Management:

  • Rest and Activity Modification: Advising on modifications to activities that may exacerbate symptoms and recommending rest.
  • Ice Therapy: Applying ice packs to reduce inflammation and alleviate pain.
  • Strengthening Exercises: Gradual introduction of exercises to strengthen the forearm muscles.
  • Stretching Exercises: Incorporating stretches to improve flexibility and reduce tension.
  • Joint Mobilization: Manual techniques to enhance joint mobility and reduce stiffness.
  • Eccentric Exercises: Specific exercises emphasizing lengthening of the forearm muscles during contraction.
